CalRecycle

January 2023 - May 2023

Consulting • Project Management • EV Financial & Feasibility Analysis

Analyzing the state of EV industry infrastructure for the storage, collection, and recycling of batteries

Produced Deliverables:
1. Recyling Center Map via Tableau: Showcases 42 EV recycling centers within the Bay Area.
2. Final Presentation: Includes research on current EV policies in the U.S., calculations for the cost of EV battery storage and recycling, cost of transportation of EV batteries via different methods, a cost calculator tool, and upcoming policies globally.
